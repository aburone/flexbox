Fexbox history

  • 2009 (-box): Only supported by Firefox 3.5+ and Safari
  • 2011 (-flexbox): Ignored by everyone but IE 10
  • 2012 (-flex): Opera 12.1 (prefixed), Firefox 17 (about:config) / 20+ (prefixless), Chrome 23+ (prefixed)
